About Me

Hi! I am a final year undergraduate student at BITS Pilani, Goa, India. I am currently pursuing my undergraduate thesis at Microsoft Research, Bangalore, India with Sunayana Sitaram and Monojit Choudhury. Previously, I have interned at the MT-NLP Lab, IIIT Hyderabad and Liveweaver, Pune.

My current research interests include (but aren’t limited to) : multilingual NLP with a focus on low resource languages and code-mixed language processing. I have also briefly dabbled with multimodal processing and look forward to exploring it in the future. For more details, feel free to reach out to me on my email :)
